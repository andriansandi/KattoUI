# ADR Template

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Title | Short decision title |
| Date | YYYY-MM-DD |
| Status | Proposed / Accepted / Deprecated / Superseded |

## Context

What is the background and the forces at play that led to this decision? Include any constraints, goals, or non-goals.

## Decision

The decision itself, stated clearly and unambiguously. Use active voice: "We will use X because Y."

## Consequences

### Positive

- What improves because of this decision?
- What risks are avoided or reduced?

### Negative / Trade-offs

- What becomes harder?
- What new risks are introduced?
- What doors are closed?

### Related Decisions

Link to RFCs, other ADRs, or future work that follows from this decision.
