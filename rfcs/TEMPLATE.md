## Summary

One or two paragraphs summarizing the proposal, its scope, and the desired outcome.

## Motivation

Why is this RFC needed? What problem does it solve? What are the consequences of not solving it?

## Proposal

The detailed proposal. Include architecture, interfaces, data flow, diagrams, and examples.

## Drawbacks

What are the costs, risks, and limitations of this proposal?

## Alternatives

What other approaches were considered? Why were they rejected?

## Adoption

What are the concrete steps to implement and roll out this proposal?

## Future Work

What is explicitly out of scope for this RFC but may be addressed later?
